Spaghetti Carbonara

Recipe Overview

This Classic Italian pasta dish is rich, creamy, and full of flavor.
It takes only 20 minutes to prepare and is perfect for a quick meal. (Serves 2 people)

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 1/2 cup grated Premesan

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al denate
  2. Cook Pancetta until crispy
  3. Mix eggs and cheese in a bowl
  4. Combine everything with drained pasta while hot
  5. Season with salt and paper

Tips

Make sure pasta is hot when you mix in the egg and cheese mixture for a creamy texture.
